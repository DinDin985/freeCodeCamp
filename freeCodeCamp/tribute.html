Background

Before we begin with this wonderful and bizzare story I need to explain some background information about what happened before the Capture The Flag Event started. But don't worry I'm not transfering you back to high school and explaining some history about WWI that no one cares about. However I will give you a quick rundown of Shia LaBeouf's campaign. It all started back in 2017 where Donald Trump was just elected president. Shia had some plans to speak out on the event and decided to go to the Museum of the Moving Image and paint on the wall "He Will Not Divide Us". Just to be clear I'm talking about The Museum of the Moving Image in New York, not The Museum of The Moving Image in London. If I had a nickel for every time there was a nickel every time there was a museum named The Moving of The Moving Image, I'd have two nickels, which isn't a lot of money, but it's weird that it happened twice. Anyways the plan was for the exhibit at the museum to run for four years, since you know an American presidency term is four years. Now you may be asking what could possibly be at this exhibit. Well along with the “He Will Not Divide Us” phrase being painted on the wall there was a camera and microphone running 24 hours, 7 days a week and most importantly Shia would frequently be at the exhibit repeating the phrase “He Will Not Divide Us”, as a side note this whole event took place outside. Now you may be thinking that this is some of the most bizzare and weird things to happen, and you’d be correct, but it gets better. From day one people came by to see the exhibit, but the internet decided to do it’s thing and troll Shia. However this is just the beginning.

TLDR: Shia ran an anti-trump message that involved a 24/7 stream and people from the internet came by and trolled Shia with racist jokes and Pepe.
